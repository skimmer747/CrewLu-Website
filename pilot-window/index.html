Pilot Window

Your augmented reality flight deck companion.
See what's ahead, day or night.

Cloud Level

Never guess your clearance again.

Use the Cloud Level feature to look ahead at clouds and instantly determine if you can fly above them or need to deviate. The augmented reality overlay provides precise elevation angles relative to the horizon, helping you make safer, more efficient flight path decisions.

Star Gazer

Navigate the night sky.

Turn your night flights into an astronomical tour. Star Gazer mode identifies stars, planets, and constellations in real-time. Whether you're verifying celestial navigation points or just admiring the view from FL350, Pilot Window shows you exactly what you're looking at.

Learn While You Fly

Fun facts and precise data.

Tap on any celestial object to reveal a wealth of information. See the object's current heading and elevation angle relative to your position. Plus, enjoy curated "fun facts" about stars and planets to pass the time on those long night hauls.
