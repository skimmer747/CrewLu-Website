Cloud Level

"Do we need 20 left for weather?"

Use the Cloud Level feature to look ahead at clouds and instantly determine if you can fly above them or need to deviate. You can set the distance away from the weather using your radar or guesstimate, and you will know how many feet above or below the cloud top you will be. This will help you make safer, more efficient flight path decisions.

Star Gazer

Navigate the night sky.

Turn your night flights into an astronomical tour. Star Gazer mode identifies stars, planets, and even the ISS in real-time. Whether you're verifying celestial navigation points or just admiring the view from FL350, Pilot Window shows you exactly what you're looking at.

Learn While You Fly

Fun facts and precise data.

See what is actually visible at your current location and altitude. See the object's current heading and elevation angle relative to your position. Plus, enjoy curated "fun facts" about stars and planets to pass the time on those long night hauls.
