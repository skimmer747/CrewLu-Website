Turbometer

Measure the ride. Visualize the bumps.
Precision metering for smooth flights.

Real-Time Monitoring

Know exactly what your aircraft is experiencing.

Turbometer provides a comprehensive analysis of in-flight turbulence. View real-time Vertical Acceleration (g), track the Turbometer Score, and monitor Stability Scores.

With a rolling G-RMS display and severity timeline, you can objectively measure "light chop" vs. "moderate turbulence" and make informed decisions for passenger comfort and safety.

Track the Smooth Rides

Validation for the good days.

It's not just about the bumps. Turbometer confirms when the air is smooth, giving you a clear "Green" status. Use this data to log flight conditions or simply to appreciate the calm air.

Detailed Flight Metrics

Deep dive into your flight data.

Access a detailed dashboard of flight metrics over time. Analyze rolling G-RMS, weighted G-RMS, and stability scores across different time windows (8s, 60s). Perfect for post-flight analysis or understanding turbulence patterns.
