Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the CrewLu iOS app.

We Don't Collect Your Personal Information

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data. All data you create within the App is stored privately on your device and, if you choose, in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.

Data Storage and iCloud Sync

The App allows you to store your data, such as imported contract information and personal notes, directly on your device.

For your convenience, the App uses Apple's iCloud service to seamlessly sync your data across your personal devices. This data is stored in your private iCloud container, which we do not have access to. All data synced via iCloud is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service to provide functionality:

CheckWX API: To provide aviation weather information (METAR/TAF), the App sends requests to the CheckWX API. These requests contain location identifiers (e.g., ICAO codes) but do not include any of your personal or device information. For more information, you can review the CheckWX Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at skimmer747+crewlu@gmail.com.
