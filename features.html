CrewLu Features

Discover all the powerful features that make CrewLu the ultimate
crew management solution for iOS.

Smart Team Scheduling

CrewLu's intelligent scheduling system takes the complexity out of managing your team's time. Create recurring shifts, handle time-off requests, and ensure optimal coverage with our intuitive drag-and-drop interface.

  • Visual calendar with multiple view options
  • Automatic conflict detection
  • Shift swapping and coverage requests
  • Overtime tracking and alerts

Real-time Team Communication

Keep everyone on the same page with built-in messaging. Create team channels, send direct messages, and share important updates instantly. All communication is encrypted and stored securely.

  • Group and individual messaging
  • File and image sharing
  • Push notifications for important updates
  • Message read receipts and typing indicators

Comprehensive Task Management

Assign, track, and complete tasks efficiently. Set priorities, deadlines, and dependencies to ensure your team stays productive and nothing falls through the cracks.

  • Task assignment with due dates
  • Priority levels and status tracking
  • Subtasks and checklists
  • Task templates for recurring work

Performance Analytics & Reporting

Make data-driven decisions with comprehensive analytics. Track team performance, identify trends, and generate detailed reports for stakeholders.

  • Real-time performance dashboards
  • Custom report generation
  • Time and attendance tracking
  • Productivity metrics and KPIs

Centralized Document Hub

Store and share important documents securely. From training materials to company policies, keep everything organized and accessible to your team.

  • Secure cloud storage
  • Version control and history
  • Permission-based access
  • Full-text search capabilities

Designed for iOS

CrewLu is built from the ground up for iOS, taking full advantage of the latest iPhone and iPad capabilities. Experience seamless performance, intuitive gestures, and beautiful design.

  • Native iOS app with offline support
  • Face ID and Touch ID authentication
  • Apple Watch companion app
  • Dark mode support
