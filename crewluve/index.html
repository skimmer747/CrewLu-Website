Crewluve

Your family's window into the pilot's schedule.

Stay Connected

Crewluve is a companion app to CrewLu that lets family members view the pilot's schedule in an easy-to-read format. Know when they're home, when they're flying, and what's coming up next.

Seamless Sync

Schedule data is automatically shared from the pilot's CrewLu app. No manual entry required - just install and go.

Privacy First

All data stays on your devices and in your private iCloud account. No external servers, no third-party access.
